Intro

Hi! I'm Ryu and I make some cool iOS widgets and wallpapers which required XenHTML. So yea, basically widgets!

I saw some cool stuffs from Evelynam and she inspired me to create some stuffs on my own. At first I just mod her widgets to fits my setup, but later I started making stuffs using LPP and porting it to SBHTML and making deb for those who don't have LPP to use.

Then I saw some people request wa wallpaper which uses HTML for Mobile and I started made that for XenHTML and a lot of people are happy Support it, which also makes me happy. Then I started making animated wallpapers for XenHTML.

This is becoming some kind of Diary isn't it? Well this is an intro to me on this field. Hope you guys will use my widgets and wallpapers and love it!
